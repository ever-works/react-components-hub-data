## Overview

Jspreadsheet (formerly jExcel) provides a lightweight and responsive spreadsheet component for web applications, offering a free MIT-licensed Lite version alongside commercial tiers.

## Features

- Formula support
- CSV import and export
- Mobile-friendly responsive design
- Lightweight footprint
- Spreadsheet-like editing experience
- Custom cell rendering

## Pricing

- Lite: Free under MIT license
- Pro and Enterprise: Commercial licenses available
