## Overview

SendGrid is an email delivery platform operated by Twilio, providing APIs and infrastructure for sending transactional and marketing emails at scale. It offers extensive tools for email management, analytics, and template design.

## Features

- Scalable email infrastructure for millions of sends
- Dynamic transactional templates with Handlebars syntax
- Marketing campaigns with drag-and-drop email builder
- Email validation and verification API
- Comprehensive analytics and reporting dashboard
- REST API and SMTP relay for sending
- Webhooks for event tracking (delivered, opened, clicked, bounced)
- Dedicated IP addresses for enterprise senders
- Suppression management and bounce handling
- Integration support with React email libraries and workflows

## Pricing

Free tier available with limited monthly sends. Paid plans scale by email volume with dedicated IP and advanced features for enterprise use.
