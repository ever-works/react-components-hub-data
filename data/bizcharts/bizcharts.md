## Overview

BizCharts is a React-based charting library developed by Alibaba. It provides charting services for G2 (a visualization grammar-based engine) and React-based business applications. The library offers the most extensive collection of chart templates among React chart libraries.

## Features

- Extensive collection of chart templates for business visualization
- Built on top of G2, a powerful visualization grammar engine
- Highly versatile and comprehensive base for designing data visualizations
- Supports a wide variety of chart types for business analytics
- Popular in Asian markets and widely used in enterprise environments
- Approximately 20,452 weekly npm downloads
