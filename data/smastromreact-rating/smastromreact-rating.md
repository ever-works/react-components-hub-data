## Overview

Zero-dependency, highly customizable rating component for React. Pick and customize one of the 6 built-in rating shapes or create your own.

## Key Features

- Zero dependencies
- 6 built-in shapes
- Custom shapes support
- Smooth transitions
- Keyboard accessible
- Mobile-friendly
- Touch support
- Multiple sizes
- TypeScript support
- SSR compatible

## Built-in Shapes

- Star
- ThinStar
- RoundedStar
- ThinRoundedStar
- StickerStar
- Heart
