## Overview

Figma is a cloud-based design and prototyping tool that is the industry standard for UI/UX design. It enables designers and developers to collaborate in real-time on wireframes, component designs, and interactive prototypes, making it the primary tool for prototyping React components and app layouts.

## Features

- Real-time collaborative design editing
- Vector design and component-based layouts
- Interactive prototyping with transitions
- Design system management
- Plugin ecosystem
- Developer handoff with inspect mode
- Auto-layout for responsive designs
- Community templates and resources

## Pricing

Free Starter plan available. Professional plans start at $12/editor/month. Organization and Enterprise plans available.
