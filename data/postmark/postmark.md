## Overview

Postmark is a transactional email service provider focused on reliable delivery of password resets, order confirmations, and other mission-critical emails. It provides REST APIs and webhooks for integration with any application stack, including React.

## Features

- Fast transactional email delivery
- High deliverability with dedicated sending infrastructure
- Email tracking with open and click analytics
- Webhook support for event notifications
- Inbound email routing and parsing
- Pre-built templates with template language support
- API and SMTP sending options
- Bounce and spam complaint handling
- Multiple email signature support
- Integration with React email tooling like react-email

## Pricing

Offers a free tier with limited monthly sends. Paid plans scale based on email volume with dedicated IP options available for high-volume senders.
