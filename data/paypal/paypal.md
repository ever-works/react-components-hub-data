## Overview

PayPal is one of the most commonly used payment providers for React applications. It offers comprehensive APIs and React-compatible SDK libraries for integrating checkout flows, payment processing, and subscription management.

## Features

- Smart Payment Buttons for React
- PayPal Express Checkout
- Subscription and recurring billing
- Global payment method support
- Buyer and seller protection
- Fraud detection and risk management
- Webhook notification system
- Extensive developer documentation and REST APIs

## Pricing

Pay per transaction. Standard rates are typically 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction in the US, with volume discounts available.
